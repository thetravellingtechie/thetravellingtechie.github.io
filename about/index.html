Born in Niger and raised in France, I built my career across the full spectrum of IT — from development and infrastructure to cloud, cybersecurity, FinOps, and platform architecture. Then outward: program management, digital transformation strategies, advising enterprises and public institutions across continents, particularly in Africa.

In parallel, I was always teaching. At LMS IT, as a freelance instructor at other training centers, or simply one-on-one for free. Sharing knowledge has never been optional for me.

Then Canada. Montréal. The work continued — and new chapters opened: AI integration, vibe coding, agentic AI workflows. The tools evolved. The mission didn't. The rest is still being written — and we're working on it right now.
